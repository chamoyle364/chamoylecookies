About

William Moyle is the founder and CEO of Chamoyle's Cookies. He was born and raised in Brigham City, Utah. He founded Chamoyle's when he was 22 years old after making some chocolate chip cookies for some friends. The name Chamoyle comes from a nickname he was given that is a combination of the Mexican sauce, "chamoy" and his last name. He now lives in Provo where he attends Brigham Young University. His other hobbies include making music, playing Super Smash Bros. and other video games, and cooking for others.
